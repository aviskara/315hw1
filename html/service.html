Data Visualization for Animal Shelter
 During the middle of my junior year, I participated in the Code for Good Hackathon hosted by JPMorgan. In the hackathon each team is introduced to multiple non-profits which each have a problem they each would like for us to solve. Our group decided to work with the Humane Society of North Texas which requested us to create a webapp that can pull their data and display it in a nice clean fashion. Throughout the project I worked on the backend using Django. Although this was my first hackathon and I was nervous throughout, my team and I were able to pull through and overall win the hackathon.
